# 🏢 HR Module – Menu Layout

> The HR department consists of **8 major categories**. Sub-categories and programs are registered under each major category.

---

## 📋 Overview

| # | Major Category | Sub-categories |
|---|---------------|----------------|
| 1 | **HR Management** | Master Data · HR Basics · Personal Info Inquiry |
| 2 | **Employee Benefits** | Master Data · Student Loan Management |
| 3 | **Attendance Management** | Master Data · Attendance Management |
| 4 | **Payroll Management** | Master Data · Payroll Base · Calculation · Output · Retroactive · Wage Increase Sim. |
| 5 | **Settlement Management** | Year-End Tax · Retirement Settlement · Estimated Severance Pay |
| 6 | **Short-Term Contract** | Short-Term Contract |
| 7 | **Withholding Tax** | Withholding Tax · Business/Other/Dividend Income · Resident Tax Filing |
| 8 | **Self-Service** | Personal Info Inquiry · Application Form · Application Management |

---

## 1️⃣ HR Management

```
HR Management
├── Master Data           → Manage code-based data & department info
│   ├── Other Code Registration
│   ├── Department Registration
│   ├── HR Master
│   ├── Personnel Record Card
│   ├── Organization Chart Inquiry
│   └── Batch Appointment Registration
│
├── HR Basics             → Manage employee personnel information
│   ├── HR Master
│   ├── Personnel Record Card
│   ├── Organization Chart Inquiry
│   ├── Batch Appointment Registration
│   ├── Comprehensive HR Status
│   ├── New Hires & Exits Inquiry
│   ├── Education/Address Inquiry, etc.
│   └── HR Status Report
│
└── Personal Info Inquiry → View employee info by management item
    ├── Comprehensive HR Status
    ├── New Hires & Exits Inquiry
    ├── Education/Address Inquiry, etc.
    └── HR Status Report
```
